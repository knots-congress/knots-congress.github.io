The First International On-line Knot Theory Congress

Feb 1 - Feb 5 (UTC+0), 2025 by Zoom

In honor of the 80th birthday of Louis H. Kauffman

This on-line congress is an opportunity to share current research results on knot theory from around the world. All talks will be on-line over zoom, with zoom links available on this webpage before the conference. Questions about the conference can be directed to any members of the organising committee.
